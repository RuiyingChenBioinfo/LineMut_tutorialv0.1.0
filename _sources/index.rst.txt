=========================================

**Release: v0.1.0**

Introduction
---------------

We introduce **LineMut**, an open-source framework for lineage reconstruction from spatial omics data through a spatially informed aggregation unit, **CellMetaBin (CMB)**. By aggregating spots into well-defined, spatially coherent meta-units, CMB preserves read-level nucleotide evidence while improving SNV detectability and maintaining spatial specificity. LineMut integrates CMB-based SNV recovery (**LineMut Call**) with downstream lineage inference (**LineMut Build**), providing a practical route for *in situ* reconstruction of clonal relationships in diverse plant tissues.
